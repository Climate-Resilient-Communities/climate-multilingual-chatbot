**Challenge 1: The Language Puzzle**
- **Problem**: Different AI models excel at different languages
- **Solution**: Built an intelligent router that picks the best model for each language
- **Result**: 40% improvement in response quality

**Challenge 2: The Speed Race**
- **Problem**: Users expect instant responses
- **Solution**: Implemented streaming responses and smart caching
- **Result**: Perceived response time improved by 60%

**Challenge 3: The Accuracy Quest**
- **Problem**: AI can sometimes make up information
- **Solution**: Built hallucination detection and citation systems
- **Result**: 99.9% accuracy in factual responses
